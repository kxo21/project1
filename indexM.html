PATRICK LAVON MAHOMES II

He attended Texas Tech University for 3 years where he threw for 93 TD's on just 11,200 yards with a 63.5% completion percentage. He also rushed for 1,934 yards while adding 26 TD's on the ground.

In 2015, his second season at Tech, Mahomes completed 364 passes on 573 attempts which resulted in 4,653 passing yards and 36 TD's. He also rushed for 456 yards with 10 rushing touchdowns. Although Tech did not accomplish much this year. Mahomes showed his force with notable wins against Sam Houston State and UTEP. Mahomes was also apart of the Red Raider baseball team but did did not see much action, appearing in only 3 games.

In his final year at Texas Tech, Mahomes decided to give up baseball and focus soley on football as he positions himself for the NFL. On October 22, 2016 he set a NCAA, Big-12 record for most overall yards in a single game with 819, of that 734 coming by way of passing yards. This was against a Baker Mayfield led Oklahoma team who also was on a tear tha game. Both teams combined for a total of 125 points for the most combined points ina single college game. That season, Mahomes led the nation with an average of 421 yards per game. He ended the seaso with a total of 5,052 passing yards and 53 total touchdowns.

The following year, Mahomes entered th NFL draft and was selected 10th overall (just ahead of Watson) by the Kansas City Chiefs. In his rookie season, he did not get much of any action sitting and learning behind Alex Smith for the year. However, he did see burn in the final game of the seaon completing 22/35 passes for 284 yards. In 2018, his first full season as a starting quarterback, Mahomes certainly proved his worth all year long. In this season Mahomes went on to win the NFL MVP award along with the Best NFL Player ESPY Award. He became only the second QB in NFL history along with Peyton Manning to throw for 5000 yards and 50 touchdowns in a single season. In 2019 Mahomes became a Super Bowl Champion. He led the Chiefs to a 24-0 comeback victory against he Texans in the second round of the playoffs going on a 51-7 run to win it 51-31. In Super Bowl LIV the Chiefs found themselves trailing 20-10 agsinst the 49ers in the fourth quarter. Once again, Mahomes proved victourious with a 21-0 skunk to defeat the 49ers with just 8:53 left in the game. Mahomes was named Super Bowl MVP, throwing for 286 yards and 2 TD's.

As of July 6th, Mahomes signed the biggest contract in league history when he agreed on a 10-year $503 million dollar deal with the Kansas City Chiefs! $$$
